    Week profit report¶ ↑

    Summary¶ ↑

    Ask the user for the revenue and expenses for each day of the week, Monday through Sunday inclusive, and print a pretty table with the revenue, expenses, and profit for each day of the week, as well as the total revenue, expenses, and profit for the week, and the average revenue, expenses, and profit for the week.

    Example output¶ ↑

    |=====|=========|==========|=========|
| Day | Revenue | Expenses | Profit  |
|-----|---------|----------|---------|
| Mon | $  6.00 | $   3.00 | $  3.00 |
| Tue | $  3.00 | $   1.00 | $  2.00 |
| Wed | $  4.00 | $   2.00 | $  2.00 |
| Thu | $  7.00 | $   7.00 | $  0.00 |
| Fri | $  9.00 | $   1.00 | $  8.00 |
| Sat | $  3.00 | $   2.00 | $  1.00 |
| Sun | $  2.00 | $   0.00 | $  2.00 |
|-----|---------|----------|---------|
| Tot | $ 34.00 | $  16.00 | $ 18.00 |
|-----|---------|----------|---------|
| Avg | $  4.86 | $   2.29 | $  2.57 |
|=====|=========|==========|=========|
